⚖️  AMICUS AI — ATTORNEY QUICK-START GUIDE
================================================================


1 — GETTING STARTED
--------------------

STEP 1 — Open Amicus
  Double-click the Amicus shortcut on your desktop, or run
  launch.sh from the Amicus folder. Your browser will open
  automatically.

STEP 2 — Create your case
  Click "New Case" in the left sidebar. Enter the case name
  (e.g. "Smith v. Jones"). Click Create.

STEP 3 — Upload your documents
  Click "Upload Documents" in the sidebar. Select your PDF,
  Word, or text files. Amicus will process them — this takes
  1–2 minutes per document.


2 — ASKING QUESTIONS
---------------------

Type your question in the search box exactly as you would ask
a colleague. Examples:

  • "What did the defendant say about the contract signing date?"
  • "Are there any contradictions between the witness statements?"
  • "What damages are being claimed and how were they calculated?"
  • "Summarize all accommodation requests made by the plaintiff."


3 — READING YOUR RESULTS
-------------------------

Amicus shows its answer with the exact source documents cited.
Always verify important facts against the original documents
before relying on them in court.

The confidence badge (High / Medium / Low) reflects how well the
answer matches the source documents. High confidence means the
cited sources directly support the answer.

The Sources section lists every document Amicus consulted to
build its answer, with a relevance score.


4 — ANALYSIS MODES
-------------------

  Basic    — Fast answers. Best for simple lookups.

  Advanced — More thorough. Recommended for most questions.

  Expert   — Deepest analysis. Best for complex multi-document
             questions. Takes approximately 30 seconds longer.


5 — PRIVACY NOTE
----------------

Your files stay on your computer. Amicus runs entirely on your
computer. Your case files are never uploaded to the internet or
shared with any third party. All analysis happens locally.
